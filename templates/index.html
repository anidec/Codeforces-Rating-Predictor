About The Project

A huge amount of your nerve cells die every time when you think what will be your future rating in the upcoming contests. Stop this! From now you could use this service, it calculates approximate upcoming rating for every contestant. This Project mainly consists of 3 Parts: Firstly, takes username as an input from the user. Then the next step is to fetch the details of the user from codeforcesAPI and uses this information as input for our ML model.Then the final step is predict the future rating and display it along with all the necessary information extracted from codforcesAPI.
